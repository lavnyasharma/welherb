Our Roots
Our roots are over 110 years old and span 4 generations. The origin of these all-natural remedies goes back all the way to Devi Das Sharma (1895-1984), a self-taught Vaid who was from the first batch of practitioners who were officially recognised as Ayurvedic Vaids by the then government. His dedication to helping others knew no bounds. With unwavering resolve, he developed key medicines for arthritis, eyes, jaundice, body aches, fever, hives, heart conditions, STDs, & skin allergies. Remaining true to his calling, he happily served his community till the end of his days.

His learnings are carried on by his son, RMP-licensed Vaid Om Prakash Sharma, who has pursued Ayurveda for his community’s welfare. Before he was hindered by old age, he prepared medicines with his own hands to bring the goodness of health to those in need. For over six decades, he has treated thousands, driven by a vision to help people live healthier, happier lives. In fact, he continues to do so to date.

It is in this rich soil of compassion and expertise that Welherb grew its roots. We are the manifestation of their lifelong commitment to helping people discover the path of better living. We wish to continue their legacy by being a source of health, happiness and hope to you.

For us, your well-being matters above everything else.
