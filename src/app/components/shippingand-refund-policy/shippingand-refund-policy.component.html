Shipping

We ship across India from our facility in Mohali, Punjab.

What are the charges applicable on shipping for prepaid and COD orders?

Shipping is free on all prepaid orders of ₹499 and above (post discounts). Orders below ₹499 incur a ₹70 shipping fee. COD orders carry an additional charge of ₹70 along with shipping charges, reflected at checkout. COD orders above ₹499 have free shipping.

Dispatch Timeline & Delivery

When will my order be delivered?

Orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours (Monday–Saturday, excluding holidays). We have partnered with reliable courier partners to ensure the timely delivery of your order. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location and external factors like weather, strikes, or high courier loads.

Why is my order delayed?

While we do our best to ensure that your order is delivered within the tentative timeline, there might be a delay due to logistical challenges. In the event of a delay due to factors outside of our control, we request that you reach out to us at {{ getSupportEmail() }}, and we shall strive to do our best to help you.

What if I am unavailable at the time of delivery?

Our logistics partner will attempt to contact you prior to delivery. If unreachable, a second attempt will be made. If the order cannot be delivered after two or three attempts (subject to the policies of the delivery partner), it will be returned to our facility, and the order will stand cancelled.

How can I track my order?

We share tracking details once the order is dispatched via email and WhatsApp. You can also track your parcel anytime via the Track Your Order page using your order ID. You can also track your order via the link/AWB shared on WhatsApp & email.

What if the parcel I received looks damaged/opened at the time of delivery?

If the parcel looks damaged or opened, do not accept the delivery. Return the parcel after taking a video/photo of the parcel and call us at {{ getSupportPhone() }} in the presence of the delivery agent.

How can I cancel my order?

Before dispatch: It is possible to cancel an order before it has been shipped. You can directly cancel an order if the order has not been shipped from our warehouse. To process the cancellation, simply go to the My Orders page, click on the order you wish to cancel, and click on 'Cancel Order'. You will receive a confirmation via email and WhatsApp, confirming your cancellation.

After Dispatch: Once the AWB has been generated and the order has been shipped, you will not be able to cancel the order directly. If you still wish to cancel the order, we request you to reach out to us within 24 hours at {{ getSupportEmail() }}, and we will do our best to help you with the cancellation. You will receive a cancellation confirmation via email once our team has processed it.

What happens if I refuse the delivery?

In case the order is refused at the doorstep, it will be marked for RTO (Return to Origin). The refund process is initiated only after we receive and validate the products.

Is it possible to return or exchange the products after delivery?

Due to the nature of our products, returns or exchanges are not accepted unless:

  • The product is damaged in transit
  • A wrong/incomplete/expired product is delivered

In such cases, report the issue to {{ getSupportEmail() }} within 24 hours with:

  • A video showing the defect
  • Clear photos of the received package

We will arrange a reverse pickup and evaluate the request on a case-by-case basis. Kindly do not send products back without due process. We will not accept unverified returns.

What are the necessary conditions for return/exchange?

  • Items must be unused, sealed, and in original packaging (including outer box, freebies, etc.).
  • Return requests for allergies, or natural variations in texture or colour, will not be accepted. The medicines are made with 100% Ayurvedic ingredients that are prone to natural variation over time.
  • Returns are not accepted if the seal is broken or the product is tampered with.

How long will it take to process my return/replacement request?

Once approved, refunds/replacements, as applicable, will be processed within 10–15 business days.

If my return/replacement request is validated, can I get another SKU/item as a replacement instead of the original item ordered?

A replacement or exchange request is only valid for the exact same item. One item cannot be replaced by another item of your choice during this process.

When will I receive the refund for a cancelled order?

Orders cancelled before dispatch: You shall receive the refund within 8–10 business days to the original source.

Orders cancelled after dispatch: Once the cancellation is processed and the shipment is marked for RTO by our delivery partner, we are able to process the refund within 8-10 days.

COD Orders: Our team will collect your bank details and process the refund within 10-14 business days.

For Doorstep Cancellation: Only after the order is returned to us and the products have been validated, the refund is initiated, processed and returned within 10-14 days.

For Validated Product Issues (after receiving and inspecting the returned product): If the claim is approved, your refund will be processed within 8–10 business days and refunded to the original source of payment. If the claim is rejected, the item will be returned back to you.
