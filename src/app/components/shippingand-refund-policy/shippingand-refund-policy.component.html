Shipping and Refund Policy

We offer a seamless ordering experience with secure payment options. All orders are processed immediately upon payment confirmation. Our payment gateway is encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant to ensure your financial information remains secure. You can pay using credit/debit cards, net banking, digital wallets, and other available payment methods. For any payment-related queries, please reach out to our customer support team.

Orders placed during business hours are confirmed within a few minutes. You will receive a confirmation email with your order number and details. Please keep this for your records. If you do not receive a confirmation email within 5 minutes, please check your spam folder or contact us immediately.

Shipping

1. Do you ship pan-India?

We ship across India from our facility in Mohali, Punjab.

2. How to check if my area pin code is serviceable?

To check if your area is serviceable, please enter your pin code at checkout.

3. What are the charges applicable on shipping for prepaid and COD orders?

Shipping is free on all prepaid orders of ₹499 and above (post discounts). Orders below ₹499 incur a ₹70 shipping fee. COD orders carry an additional charge of ₹70 along with shipping charges, reflected at checkout. COD orders above ₹499 have free shipping.

Dispatch Timeline & Delivery

1. When will my order be delivered?

Orders are dispatched within 24-48 hours (Monday - Saturday, excluding holidays). We have partnered with reliable courier partners to ensure the timely delivery of your order. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days, depending on your location and external factors like weather, strikes, or high courier loads.

2. Why is my order delayed?

While we do our best to ensure that your order is delivered within the tentative timeline, there might be a delay due to logistical challenges. In the event of a delay due to factors outside of our control, we request that you reach out to us at {{ getSupportEmail() }}, and we shall strive to do our best to help you.

3. What if I am unavailable at the time of delivery?

Our logistics partner will attempt to contact you prior to delivery. If unreachable, a second attempt will be made. If the order cannot be delivered after two or three attempts (subject to the policies of the delivery partner), it will be returned to our facility, and the order will stand cancelled.

4. How can I track my order?

We share tracking details once the order is dispatched via email and WhatsApp. You can also track your parcel anytime via the Track Your Order page using your order ID. You can also track your order via the link/AWB shared on WhatsApp & email.

5. What if the parcel I received looks damaged/opened at the time of delivery?

If the parcel looks damaged or opened, do not accept the delivery. Return the parcel after taking a video/photo of the parcel and call us at {{ getSupportPhone() }} in the presence of the delivery agent.

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement if the order has not been dispatched. Once an order is dispatched, cancellation is not possible. To cancel your order, please contact our customer support team at {{ getSupportEmail() }} or call {{ getSupportPhone() }}. If we receive your cancellation request within the stipulated time, a full refund will be initiated. Refunds typically reflect in your account within 5-7 business days, depending on your bank.

If the cancellation is requested after the order has been dispatched, the order will proceed with delivery, and you can refuse the delivery or initiate a return after receipt.

We offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy. Products can be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery if they are in original, unused condition with all original packaging and tags intact. The product must not show any signs of wear or damage. To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our support team with your order ID and reason for return. We will provide you with a return label and instructions. Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund or new product shipment accordingly.

Items that are damaged due to misuse, worn out, or have been used are not eligible for return or exchange. Customized or personalized items cannot be returned or exchanged.

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. The refund amount will be credited to the original payment method used during purchase. For prepaid orders, refunds include the full product price. For COD orders, the refund will include the product price but will not include the shipping and COD charges that were collected. If you have any questions about your refund status, please contact us, and we will be happy to assist you.

Please note that in some cases, refunds may take up to 15 business days depending on your bank's processing time. If you do not receive your refund within the stipulated timeframe, please reach out to us immediately.
